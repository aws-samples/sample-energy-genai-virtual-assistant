# Agent Test Scenarios

This document contains sample utterances for testing the Energy Virtual Agent, covering all possible scenarios.

## Energy Consumption Tasks

### EnergyConsumptionFunction

1. "What was my energy consumption from January 1, 2025 to January 31, 2025?"
2. "How much energy did I use last month?" 
3. "Can you tell me the cost of my energy usage for the past week?" 
4. "What's my energy consumption and cost for the second month of 2025?"
5. "What are my power usage trends over the last quarter?"

## Account Tasks

#### AccountOpenFunction

1. "I'd like to open a new account."
2. "Can you help me set up an electricity account for my new home?"
3. "What do I need to do to become a new customer?"
4. "I'm moving to a new address and need to start service."
5. "How can I register for a new electricity account?"

#### GetAccountInfoFunction

1. "Can you give me the details of my account?"
2. "I need to verify my account information."
3. "Could you tell me what address is listed on my account?"
4. "What's the meter number associated with my account?"

#### AccountUpdateFunction

1. "I need to update my phone number."
2. "Can you change my email address on file?" 
3. "I've moved. How do I update my service address?"
4. "Could you update my billing address?"

#### AccountCloseFunction

1. "I need to close my account."
2. "How do I terminate my service?"
3. "I'm moving out and need to end my electricity service."
4. "What's the process for closing my account?"
5. "Can you help me shut off my service at the end of this month?"

## Billing Tasks

#### GetBillingHistory

1. "Can I get my billing history for the last 6 months?"
2. "What were my electricity charges in January?" 
3. "I need a record of all my bills from last year."
4. "How much did I pay for electricity in the summer months?"
5. "Can you show me my payment history for the past year?"

#### BillingPayFunction

1. "I want to pay my last bill." 
2. "Can I make a payment on my account?" 
3. "How do I pay my outstanding balance?"
4. "I'd like to settle my latest electricity bill."
5. "I'd like to process a payment for $150 on my account."

## Service Ticket Tasks

#### CreateTicketFunction

1. "I need to report a power outage."
2. "Can you create a service ticket for tree branches touching power lines?" 
3. "I'd like to schedule a meter inspection."
4. "My bill seems unusually high, can you create an investigation ticket?" 
5. "I need to report a damaged electrical pole." 

#### GetTicketInfoFunction

1. "What's the status of my service ticket?"
2. "Can you check the progress of my service ticket?"
3. "When will my reported outage be fixed?"
4. "I want to check the details of my recent service request."
5. "Has anyone been assigned to my ticket yet?"

#### GetAccountTicketsFunction

1. "Show me all my open service tickets."
2. "What service requests have I made in the last month?"
3. "I need a list of all my past service tickets."
4. "How many active tickets do I have?"
5. "Can you show me the history of all my service requests?"

## Complex Scenarios

1. "I'm moving to a new address. Can you close my current account and open a new one?"
2. "What was my energy consumption last month, and how much did it cost me?"
3. "I need to update my email address and pay my latest bill."
4. "Can you give me my account details and create an outage service ticket?"
